Hi!

My name is Erin Brady, and I am currently a Software Development apprentice at Year Up, a nonprofit organization that works to close the wealth and skills gaps by connecting young adults from low-opportunity backgrounds to companies that need professional talent in technology, business, and finance. I develop my coding skills in JavaScript, Ruby, Python, and Java 8.5 hours per day, 5 days a week with instructors from the New York Code and Design Academy. I am on track to begin a software internship in February 2020.

I achieved my Associate of Science degree in Multimedia Programming and Design in August 2019 at Borough of Manhattan Community College in New York City. I graduated with a 3.65 GPA and participated in the ACM-W (Association for Computing Machinery's Council on Women) chapter of the Programming club.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring the city's art and science museums and exhibits, engaging in social justice events, and getting out of town for adventures in the great outdoors.

I hope you enjoy my work and please feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, Twitter and Instagram! I appreciate all types of feedback.
