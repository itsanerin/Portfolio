OH HIROSHIMA

During the month of May 2019, I redesigned and built a new website for the Swedish band, Oh Hiroshima, in collaboration with classmates Devon Warren and Nicholas Santos.

The band's originial website was a single page site with only the band's logo and social media links.

The redesign implements content found across their social media sites to showcase their new album, provide information about the band, act as a hub to access streaming and download links for each of their albums and EP's, and enable fans to shop for their merchandise.
