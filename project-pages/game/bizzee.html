BIZZEE BEE

From September through December 2018, I designed and built an endless runner browser game, Bizzee Bee, in which the player must collect as many points or "pollen" as possible whilst avoiding contact with the enemies or "hummingbirds."

I created Bizzee Bee using the p5.js, p5.play, and p5.sound JavaScript libraries. I also utilized art tools such as Piskel and Adobe Photoshop. All game art and sound effects were created by me, while the background music was composed by Jerimiah Black and the player death music was provided by copyc4t on freesound.org.
