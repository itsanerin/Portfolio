TRINITY

From May through June 2019, I designed and built the space shooter role-playing game, Trinity, in which the player must navigate and survive various gameplay modes such as a narrative story, asteroid shooter, and boss fight in order to win.

I created Trinity using the p5.js and p5.play JavaScript libraries. I also utilized Adobe Photoshop for the game art.
