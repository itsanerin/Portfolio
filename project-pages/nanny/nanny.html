NANNY & ME

From February to June 2019, I developed a demo website for a hypothetical nonprofit, Nanny & Me, in collaboration with classmates Jessica Jumelles, Chinonso Obidike, and Jocelyn Rodriguez as a part of the Women in Technology of New York (WiTNY) Research Project Grant.

Our research found that New York City has several "High Child Care Need" areas, which are charaterized by high levels of poverty and low availability of affordable child care. As a solution, we had an idea for a web app that would act as a location-based search tool for families to find local nannies in their areas.

The website utilizes some basic functions of Google Firebase to allow for account creation and user reviews. As this is a starting demo of the app, the location-based search is unavailable at this time.
